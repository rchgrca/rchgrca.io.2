  • In early 2008, I left Yahoo! to co-found my own software company. The original idea was to provide live coverage of smaller sports events via a mobile web browser. I currently play both development and fundraising roles. Through several pivots, we transformed into Blyve: A Live Q&A Platform.

  • The Live Q&A Platform brings together groups from 1 to 1000+ to attend Q&A events hosted on your website. Blyve Q&A sessions can be led by a single subject matter expert or a panel of experts. Different than Live Chats or Webinars, Blyve's Q&A Platform contains advanced moderation and curation features to protect the integrity of your content and brand. With Blyve, you can engage your audience in real-time, reply to questions, add media and polls, insert coupons, and assign questions to panelists.

  • I took Blyve from idea, to prototype, to fundraising, to development, to launch, to promotion, to revenue, to industry recognition.
